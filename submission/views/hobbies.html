My Hobbies & Interests

Lacrosse

I enjoy lacrosse because it challenges me physically while also opening up collaboration and team building opportunities. Playing club lacrosse at CU Boulder has taught me discipline with being on time and respecting other people's time as well. The sport combines athleticism with strategy, making every game an exciting experience.

Cooking

I enjoy cooking a lot because you get to be super creative and there are millions of possibilities to explore. I think that it also teaches you multitasking as you have a ton of things to manage at once. Experimenting with different cuisines and techniques keeps it exciting and rewarding.

Volunteering

Volunteering at organizations like Park Hill Food Pantry and Epworth Foundation has been incredibly rewarding. It allows me to give back to the community and connect with people from diverse backgrounds. These experiences have taught me empathy and the importance of service to others.
